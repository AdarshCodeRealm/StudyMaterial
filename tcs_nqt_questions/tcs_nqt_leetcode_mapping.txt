STUDY TIPS
-------------------------------------------------

1. Focus on understanding the algorithm concepts rather than memorizing solutions.
2. Practice multiple variations of each problem type.
3. Time yourself when solving problems to improve speed.
4. Review solutions after solving to find more optimal approaches.
5. Practice both coding and aptitude sections of TCS NQT.
